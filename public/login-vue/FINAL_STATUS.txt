HOW IT WORKS
================================================================================

1. You enter username/password in Vue form
2. Vue fetches login token from Moodle
3. Vue creates hidden form with credentials + token
4. Form submits to Moodle's /login/index.php
5. Moodle authenticates you
6. Moodle creates session
7. Moodle redirects you to /my/ dashboard
8. You're logged in!

Same process as the PHP login, just with Vue UI!
